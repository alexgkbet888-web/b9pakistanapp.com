Pakistan wallet-evidence case

Handle an EasyPaisa or JazzCash name mismatch carefully

A wallet receipt may show a different spelling, masked name or account label. Treat that as a verification issue and preserve evidence; do not claim a payment outcome until the platform confirms it.

Match the identifiers

Compare the phone number ending, transaction ID, amount and timestamp rather than relying on the display name alone.

Protect the receipt

Redact balance and unrelated personal details before sharing a screenshot with verified support.

Wait for confirmation

Keep pending, failed and completed states separate and avoid repeating a transfer while status is unclear.

Should users post receipts publicly?

No. Keep receipts private and share only necessary evidence through verified support.
